# Future of Humanoid Robotics

This chapter explores the current trajectories and potential futures of humanoid robotics. We discuss emerging technologies, potential applications, and societal implications.

## Technological Trends

- Improved actuators and materials
- Advanced AI and machine learning
- Better human-robot interfaces
- Enhanced autonomy

## Future Applications

- Domestic assistance
- Healthcare support
- Industrial collaboration
- Education and research
- Entertainment and social interaction
