# Introduction to Physical AI

Physical AI refers to the intersection of artificial intelligence and physical systems. It encompasses the development of AI algorithms and techniques that enable machines to interact with and operate in the physical world effectively.

## What is Physical AI?

Physical AI combines traditional AI approaches with robotics, sensors, actuators, and control systems to create intelligent agents that can perceive, reason, and act in physical environments.

## Key Concepts

- Embodied cognition
- Sensorimotor learning
- Real-world interaction
- Adaptive behavior
