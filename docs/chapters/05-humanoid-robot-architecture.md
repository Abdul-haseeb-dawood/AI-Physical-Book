# Humanoid Robot Architecture

Humanoid robots are designed with human-like characteristics and form factors. This chapter explores the architectural considerations and design principles for humanoid robots.

## Design Principles

### Anthropomorphic Design
- Human-like proportions and appearance
- Biomechanical considerations
- Psychological acceptance

### Modular Architecture
- Reusable components
- Easy maintenance and upgrades
- Scalability

## Key Components

- Head with vision systems
- Torso with upper body actuators
- Arms with dexterous hands
- Legs for locomotion
- Central control system
