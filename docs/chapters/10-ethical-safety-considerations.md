# Ethical & Safety Considerations

As robots become more integrated into society, ethical and safety considerations become paramount. This chapter examines the challenges and frameworks for responsible robotics development.

## Safety Challenges

- Physical safety
- Cybersecurity
- Behavioral safety
- Fail-safe mechanisms

## Ethical Frameworks

- Asimov's laws (historical perspective)
- Contemporary ethical guidelines
- Transparency and explainability
- Privacy considerations
