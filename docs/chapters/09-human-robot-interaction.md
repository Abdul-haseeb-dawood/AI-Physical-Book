# Human-Robot Interaction

Human-robot interaction (HRI) is a multidisciplinary field focusing on the design and evaluation of robots for human use. This chapter covers principles and techniques for effective HRI.

## Key Considerations

- Safety and trust
- Natural communication modalities
- Social cues and norms
- Adaptive behavior
- Ethical considerations

## Interaction Modalities

- Speech and natural language
- Gesture and body language
- Visual interfaces
- Haptic feedback
